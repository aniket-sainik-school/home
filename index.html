ABOUT ANIKET SAINIK SCHOOL

Established in 2014, Nurturing Excellence for Over Decades

Our Story

Aniket Sainik School stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the heart of the village. Founded by Mr. Arjun Kalnu Gawai (President of Aniket Sainik School, Lakshya Carrier Academy, NAFS Engineering College, Urdu Junior College) with a vision to create future leaders, our institution combines rigorous academics with character development, physical training, and moral values.

We believe in holistic development where every student is encouraged to explore their potential, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible citizens of tomorrow. Our unique approach integrates military-style discipline with modern educational practices.

OUR VISION

To be a world-class educational institution that produces intellectually capable, physically fit, and morally upright individuals who will serve as leaders in various walks of life and contribute to nation-building.

OUR MISSION

To provide quality education that develops the whole child - intellectually, emotionally, socially, and physically. We foster a love for learning, instill discipline, and prepare students for the challenges of the modern world.

CORE VALUES

Integrity - Honesty in all actions Excellence - Striving for the best Discipline - Self-control and order Respect - For self and others Courage - To face challenges

üìú School Philosophy

"Education shapes who we become, and its benefits stay for life."

At Aniket Sainik School, we believe that every child is unique and possesses immense potential. Our philosophy centers on discovering and nurturing this potential through a balanced approach of academics, co-curricular activities, sports, and character education. We prepare students not just for examinations, but for life itself.
