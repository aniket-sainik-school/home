Our Story
Aniket Sainik School stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the heart of the village. Founded by - Mr.Arjun Kalnu Gawai(President of Aniket Sainik School, Lakshya Carrier Academy, NAFS Engineering College, Urdu Junior College) Founded with a vision to create future leaders, our institution combines rigorous academics with character development, physical training, and moral values.

We believe in holistic development where every student is encouraged to explore their potential, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible citizens of tomorrow. Our unique approach integrates military-style discipline with modern educational practices.
